Lasagna

This is an Italian dish especially loved by yeankee orange cats and can basically be described as a pasta-cake

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Meat or textured soy
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Oregan
  • Spices of choice
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and fry them on a pan with just a little bit of oil and a pinch of salt
  2. Once the onions are looking transparent and soft, add the garlic, which should have previously been cut in tiny bits
  3. Fry the garlic and the onions for aproximatelly a minute before adding the meat
  4. Immediatly after adding the meat, spice the mix as you like, I recomment using pepper and curry
  5. Once everything is cooked enough, turn of the stove and oil the platter where the lasagna will be made
  6. Cover the platter with slices of pasta and then cover the pasta with some of the mix, repeat until you run out of pasta or fill the full platter
  7. Add the cheese on top
  8. Cook on the oven for aproximatelly 20 minutes at full temperature
  9. Enjoy
