Steps

  3. Make sure theres no pasta not fully submerged, just pay some attention, willya?

  5. Taste the tip of one when you think it's done to be sure, just don't wait too long or it'll lose all flavour and texture
  6. Get it out of the water and in a plate
  7. You better have done a sauce to go with it, otherwise it's gonna be pretty plain
  8. Enjoy I guess, I hope you remembered to turn off the stove
