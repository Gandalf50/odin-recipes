Pizza

This recipe will teach you the basic procedure to make a homemade pizza that you can use to surprise your friends, idk, just learn how to make pizza, who wouldn't want to learn that?

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Levadura
  • Oil
  • Idk, other stuff

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Divide it into how many pizzas you want, save a fist-sized piece of dough for each pizza
  3. Wait for it to grow, at least an hour
  4. Give each piece of dough the size you want your pizza to be and throw it in the oven
  5. Once it's cooked - looks brownish - get it out of the oven, turn it upside down, throw some souce on top and add some cheese, then throw it back in the oven
  6. Repeat this proces with each pizza
  7. Once the cheese is all melted down, take the pizza out of the oven and throw it on some nice flat surface - not the floor - so you can cur it out on how many slices you want
  8. If you wanna add some other ingredients on top like pineapple - why not, right? - you should do so before cutting the slices so that it's easier to distribute everything evenly
  9. Throw your pizza to the garbage
  10. Order some good pizza from someone who knows what they're doing
  11. Enjoy
